Our Shared Diamond Philosophy

We believe youth sports should build character, not financial stress. By bringing U-M student-athletes directly into local Metro Detroit neighborhoods, we provide completely free safety gear, instruction, and a welcoming team environment for every kid.

Sustainability & Community

The Gear Donation Loop

Have old, outgrown baseball gear gathering dust in your garage? Drop it off at our premium hubs in Oakland or Washtenaw counties. We sanitize, refurbish, and distribute 100% of collected bats, gloves, and helmets directly to kids at our municipal park clinics.

Active Drop-Off Locations:

  • 📍 Oakland: Detroit Country Day School Main Athletic Office
  • 📍 Washtenaw: U-M Alumni Field Equipment Shed
